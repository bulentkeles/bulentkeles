Problem

Public Bikes is a San Francisco based bike retailer that sells a new line of electric bikes. They believe in their product, however they are having difficulty selling them in the US market. Public is looking to understand what they can do on their website to turn this around.

Solution

We created a product comparison module for new customers unaware of e-bikes. We also built a landing page explaining the benefits of an e-bike. And designed a page with curated test rides where customers could easily pick a route to test the e-bike they could easily relate to.
